About Nerogab Clothing Concepts

Bringing affordable, trendy fashion to Nigeria — one outfit at a time

Our Story

Nerogab Clothing Concepts was founded with a simple mission: to make stylish, high-quality fashion accessible to everyone in Nigeria. Based in Agbarha Otor, Delta State, we serve customers nationwide with carefully curated clothing collections for women, men, and children.

We believe fashion should empower and inspire. Every piece in our collection is selected to offer excellent value — bringing you the latest global trends at prices that make sense for the Nigerian market.

From casual everyday wear to special occasion outfits, we stock hundreds of styles across women's fashion, men's wear, accessories, footwear, and bags.

Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: January 2025 | Nerogab Clothing Concepts (RC: 8148187)

Our Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, we offer a 14-day return/exchange policy.

Eligible Returns

  • Items must be returned within 14 days of delivery
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • Items must have original tags attached
  • Sale items are eligible for exchange only, not refunds

Non-Returnable Items

  • Underwear and swimwear for hygiene reasons
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Customised or personalised items

How to Return

1Contact us via WhatsApp (+2348080868957) or email within 14 days
2Provide your order ID and photos of the item
3Wait for our confirmation and return instructions
4Ship the item to our address (buyer pays return shipping)
5Receive your refund or exchange within 5–7 working days

Refund Method

Refunds are processed back to the original payment method or via bank transfer to your account within 5–7 working days of receiving the returned item.
